About Me

After 13 years in financial services, I discovered my passion for coding. I decided to transition to web development to begin a new career providing simple content structure, clean design patterns, and thoughtful interactions.

I'm quietly confident, naturally curious, and perpetually working on improving my skills one bug at a time. When I'm not pushing pixels, I can be found sinking myself into a good book, gaming, exercising, or scouring the internet for hardware for my next PC build.

My Services

I’m dedicated to providing finished products that exceed client expectations. Knowing what to expect when we work together is critical - here is how I deliver success:

1. Consultation

A kick-off meeting where we will discuss the project scope and determine what design or functional elements are most important to you.

2. Proposal

A detailed proposal will follow, providing a visual demonstration, project workflow, and timeline according to our discussions.

3. Creation

You will receive regular updates regarding project status as it progresses.

4. Launch & Support

The final product is delivered and you can enjoy the benefits of a responsive and modern website.
